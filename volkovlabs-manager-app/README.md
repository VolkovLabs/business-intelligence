## Introduction

The Business Manager is a frontend for the Business Intelligence for Grafana.

## Requirements

- Business Manager 2.X requires Grafana 10 or Grafana 11.
- Business Manager 1.X requires Grafana 10.

## Highlights

- Business Engine.
- Business Alerting.
